# Privacy Policy — mymd

**Last updated: 2026-03-20**

## Data Collection

mymd does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information.

## What mymd accesses

- **Markdown files you open**: mymd reads the content of `.md` files you navigate to in your browser, solely to render them as formatted HTML. This content is processed entirely locally and never leaves your device.
- **User preferences**: Your settings (theme, font size, layout options) are saved locally using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage`). These preferences may sync across your Chrome devices if you have Chrome Sync enabled — this is a standard Chrome feature, not controlled by mymd.

## What mymd does NOT do

- Does not collect analytics or telemetry
- Does not track browsing history
- Does not use cookies
- Does not communicate with any external server
- Does not display advertisements
- Does not sell or share any data with third parties
